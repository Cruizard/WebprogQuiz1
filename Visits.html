Touring Spots

Maybe food's not depok's strong suit, but for tourist destinations we have a couple of great spots for you to look at:

The first one being Dewa's starfruit farm, as one of the bigges companies that produce Depok's renowned sweets, you can visit their garden and have a tour of their starfruit garden

Next up is for you wilderness lovers we have Depok's natural preserve. Here you can enjoy forest-like areas where the air is fresh and theres a lot of tall trees and greenery.

Speaking of greenery, there is also Wiladatika's flower garden, for all you flower lowers come as you visit the beautiful garden filled with a variety of flowers to look at and enjoy

Lastly, for the people who like to go and a more thrilling adventure, we have Ciliwung river rafting, group up with friends and family as you paddle your way in inflated rafts across ciliwung river. A great physical activity to have fun at to those who don't mind getting wet
